Fence Material Formula

Posts = ceil((Length − Gate Openings) ÷ Spacing) + 1

For pickets, this tool assumes tight layout with minimal gap. Add 5–10% extra for cuts and defects.

Before You Buy

  • Check local code for post depth and spacing limits.
  • Corner and gate posts often require heavier material.
  • If your yard slopes, add extra pickets and rails for step sections.
