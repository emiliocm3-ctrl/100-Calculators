Roof Bundle Formula

Bundles = ((Footprint × PitchFactor) × (1 + Waste%)) ÷ 100 × 3

Roofing is sold in squares (100 sq ft). Most architectural shingles require about 3 bundles per square.

Accuracy Notes

  • Complex roofs with many valleys need higher waste.
  • Starter strips, ridge caps, and underlayment are separate materials.
  • Always round up to full bundles.
