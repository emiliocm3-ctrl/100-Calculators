Margin vs. Markup

Margin% = (Price − Cost) ÷ Price; Markup% = (Price − Cost) ÷ Cost

These are not the same. A 40% margin corresponds to a 66.7% markup.

Why This Matters

  • Margin drives profitability and operating runway.
  • Markup helps when setting supplier-based pricing rules.
  • Track both to avoid underpricing.
